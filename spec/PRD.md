# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Vision
Build an AI-native, fully automated content production system
for high-CPM finance & economics YouTube channels,
optimized for retention, scalability, and profit.

## 2. Immutable Objectives
- Profit-first design
- Automation before human labor
- Data-driven decision making
- Agent-based modular architecture
- Scalable to multi-channel and SaaS

## 3. Success Metrics (North Stars)
- Average View Duration (AVD)
- Retention Curve
- CTR (Thumbnail + Title)
- RPM / CPM
- Time from idea → published video

## 3.1 Revenue / Retention / Automation KPI
Define channel-specific metrics to validate revenue, retention, and automation effectiveness.

### Channel-Specific Success Metrics
- Finance / Economics
  - CTR and impression share
  - AVD, 30-second retention, and retention curve slope
  - RPM / CPM and total revenue per video
  - Returning viewer rate and subscriber conversion
- Education (general)
  - CTR and impression-to-view rate
  - AVD, completion rate, and watch time per session
  - RPM and sponsor-read conversion (if applicable)
  - Playlist continuation rate
- News / Analysis
  - CTR and 24-hour view velocity
  - AVD and retention curve stability
  - RPM / CPM and ad fill rate
  - Returning viewer rate by series/topic

### Automation Efficiency Metrics
- Time from idea → published video
- Script rewrite rate per episode
- QA pass rate on first submission
- Cost per produced minute (API + compute)

## 4. Explicit Non-Goals
- Emotion-driven content
- Manual-heavy workflows
- Trend chasing without monetization
- Non-repeatable creative processes

## 5. Core Deliverables
- End-to-end automated pipeline
- Agent orchestration system
- Continuous evaluation & rewrite loops
- Multi-channel scaling support
